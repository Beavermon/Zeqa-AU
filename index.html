FAQ

How can I be staff?

Zeqa AU is currently not accepting staff applications. However, we are looking for a designer.

What perks do boosters get?

Boosters get a custom name and badge, access to a secret channel, shoutout in announcements and much more!

Do you have to be from AU/NZ to join?

No, Zeqa AU is open to everyone from everywhere to join. However, it is reccomended that you be from AU. Be sure to select reigon roles when you join!

How do I submit a cheaterlist/leaderboard submission?

Please open a ticket with the appropriate tag in our Discord. Spamming tickets will end up in a mute.
